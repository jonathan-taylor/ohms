About OHMS

The Online Homework Management System (OHMS) was started by Dennis Sun and Praveen Bommannavar in 2012. Its first users were the 180 students in the Autumn 2012 offering of Stats 60, the introductory statistics course at Stanford. Since then, the project has expanded to courses across Stanford.

OHMS combines an expressive language for authoring homework questions with a flexible backend based on Google Drive to facilitate grading and data analysis. As such, it is appropriate for homeworks that can be automatically graded and for homeworks that require manual grading (and especially those that are a mix of the two).

The OHMS project is open source and always looking for contributors. Please see our github page for more information.
